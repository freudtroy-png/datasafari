Coverage questions

A Country plan covers one specific destination end-to-end using that country's local 4G/LTE or 5G networks. Your plan activates automatically when you land. Data sizes range from 1 GB to Unlimited with validity from 3 to 90 days.
Regional bundles cover entire continents on one plan — e.g. the Europe bundle covers 40+ European countries. Global plans work across all 200+ countries worldwide. Regional suits multi-stop trips within one continent; Global is best for travellers crossing multiple regions.
