This Cookie Policy explains how DataSafari Ltd. ("DataSafari", "we", "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website at datasafari.world (the "Site"). It explains what these technologies are, why we use them, and your rights to control their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better browsing experience, and give website owners information about how the site is used. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, tags, and local storage. References to "cookies" in this policy include these equivalent technologies.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies on our Site:

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our Site. We do not control these cookies. Third parties include:

  • Analytics providers (Matomo, Plausible): Privacy-focused analytics without sharing data with other platforms.
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal): Set necessary cookies during the checkout process. Their use is governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Advertising partners (Meta, Google): Set marketing cookies only with your consent for targeted advertising and campaign measurement.

5. Your Cookie Choices and Consent

When you first visit our Site, we display a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies (necessary + preferences + analytics + marketing).
  • Reject all non-necessary cookies (only necessary cookies will be set).
  • Customise your preferences per category before accepting.

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer of our Site.

6. How to Manage Cookies via Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View and delete cookies already stored on your device.
  • Block cookies from all or specific websites.
  • Set your browser to notify you before accepting a cookie.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Site. Instructions for managing cookies by browser:

  • Chrome: Settings, Privacy and Security, Cookies and other site data.
  • Safari: Preferences, Privacy, Manage Website Data.
  • Firefox: Options, Privacy and Security, Cookies and Site Data.
  • Edge: Settings, Cookies and site permissions, Cookies and site data.

7. Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Our Site currently does not respond to Do Not Track (DNT) signals as there is no consistent industry standard for DNT interpretation. We continue to monitor developments in this area. In the meantime, you can manage cookie preferences as described in Sections 5 and 6 above.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation, or our data practices. Material changes will be notified via a cookie banner or site notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.
