At DataSafari, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds, exchanges, and cancellations are processed for DataSafari eSIM plans.

Our Promise

If your eSIM has not been activated on a mobile network in your destination, you are eligible for a full refund. No questions asked.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • The eSIM profile has not been activated (first network connection in the destination has not occurred).
  • The request is made within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • The eSIM was purchased directly from the DataSafari website or app (not through a third-party reseller).

Refunds are not available under the following circumstances:

  • The eSIM has been activated and data usage has commenced.
  • The plan validity period has expired, regardless of data usage.
  • The request is made more than 30 days after purchase.
  • The eSIM was purchased from an authorised reseller or third-party marketplace (contact them directly).
  • The refund is requested due to device incompatibility that was listed as incompatible on our website at the time of purchase.

2. How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us via live chat on our website, email at support@datasafari.world, or WhatsApp.
  2. Provide your order number and the email address used for the purchase.
  3. Confirm activation status. We will verify whether the eSIM has been activated.
  4. Refund processed. If eligible, we will issue the refund to your original payment method.

Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days. It may take additional time for the refund to appear on your payment method statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.

3. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer partial refunds at our discretion:

  • Technical issues that prevented activation but where partial network connectivity was established.
  • Plans purchased with promotional discounts or coupon codes will be refunded at the discounted amount paid.
  • Top-up data purchases on an already-activated eSIM are non-refundable once the top-up has been applied.

4. Cancellation Before Activation

You may cancel a plan at any time before the eSIM profile has been installed on your device. Cancellation before installation is eligible for a full refund per Section 1. Once the eSIM profile has been downloaded to your device but not yet activated (no network connection in destination), you remain eligible for a refund as long as no activation has occurred.

5. Technical Issues and Service Problems

If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using your eSIM as described at the point of purchase:

  • Contact our support team immediately. Most issues can be resolved within minutes.
  • If we cannot resolve the issue and the eSIM has not been used, a full refund will be issued.
  • If the issue is caused by your device, network settings, or carrier restrictions, we will work with you to diagnose but refund eligibility follows the standard policy.

6. Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank or card issuer (chargeback) before contacting us, your account may be suspended pending resolution. We encourage you to contact us first so we can resolve any issues directly. Unnecessary chargebacks may result in account closure and forfeiture of any refund eligibility.

7. Exchanges and Plan Changes

eSIM plans cannot be exchanged or transferred to a different country, region, or plan type after purchase. If you need a different plan, please purchase the correct plan and request a refund for the unused original plan per the standard policy.

8. Force Majeure

DataSafari is not liable for refunds or compensation due to events beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, network outages by partner operators, government actions, travel disruptions, or pandemics. We will evaluate such situations on a case-by-case basis and may offer goodwill credits at our discretion.

9. Promotional Plans and Giveaways

Plans obtained through promotions, giveaways, competitions, or as complementary gifts are not eligible for monetary refunds. They may be exchanged for equivalent value at our discretion.

10. Contact for Refund Requests

To request a refund or ask questions about this policy, contact us at:

Email: support@datasafari.world
Live Chat: Available on our website 24/7
WhatsApp: Available via our website
Response time: Typically under 2 minutes via chat, under 4 hours via email

We are committed to fair and transparent refund practices. If your situation is not covered by this policy, please reach out. We evaluate edge cases individually and are generally very reasonable.
