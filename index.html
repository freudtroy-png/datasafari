An eSIM is a digital SIM built into your phone. Instead of inserting a plastic card, you scan a QR code to install a data profile. It activates automatically when you arrive at your destination.
Most phones from 2019 onwards support eSIM — iPhone XS+, Samsung S20+, Google Pixel 3+, and many more. Dial *#06# on your phone; if an EID number appears, you're compatible.
