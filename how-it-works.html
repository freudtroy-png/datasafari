Installation guides

Step-by-step for
your device

The menus look slightly different by brand — but the process is the same. Always install before you travel while on Wi-Fi.



iPhone (iOS 16+)

Works on iPhone XS, XR, and all newer models

  1. Open Settings → tap Mobile Data or Cellular
  2. Tap Add eSIM → select Use QR Code
  3. Point your camera at the DataSafari QR code from your email
  4. Tap Continue and give the plan a label (e.g. "DataSafari")
  5. Set DataSafari as your default data line
  6. Enable Data Roaming — you're all set
Takes less than 2 minutes. The QR is valid for 30 days from purchase. Validity starts at first use in-country.
G

Android (Samsung / Pixel)

Works on Galaxy S20+, Pixel 3+ and compatible models

  1. Open Settings → Connections or Network & Internet
  2. Tap SIM Card Manager → Add eSIM
  3. Tap Scan QR code from carrier
  4. Point your camera at the DataSafari QR from your email
  5. Name the eSIM and set it as default for data
  6. Enable Data Roaming on the DataSafari line — done
Samsung menus say "SIM Card Manager". Pixel and other Android phones say "Network & Internet → SIMs". Both work identically.

Can't scan the QR? Every email includes a manual activation code (SM-DP+ address) you can type directly into your phone's eSIM settings. Our 24/7 support can also resend your QR instantly.
